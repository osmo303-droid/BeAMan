Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Welcome to Be A Man! This app is built for entertainment and humor. We respect your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal data from you.

  • Favorites are stored only on your device using local storage.
  • Notification preferences (time) are stored only on your device.
  • No account creation, no login, no tracking, no analytics.

2. Notifications

The app may send local push notifications (quotes) if you enable them. These are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server.

3. Third Parties

The app does not share any information with third parties. When you use the Share button, it uses your phone’s built-in sharing system.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact us via GitHub issues in this repository.

This app is a fun, satirical tribute to the "Boston Be A Man" comedy style.
Made with ❤️ for entertainment purposes only.
