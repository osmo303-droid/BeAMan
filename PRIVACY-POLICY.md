# PRIVACY POLICY

**BE A MAN** is a fun, satirical quote app made for entertainment only.

## No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require login, accounts, or any form of registration.

## Local Storage Only

Your favorite quotes and notification settings are saved only on your device using `localStorage`. Nothing is sent to any server.

## Notifications

If you enable daily notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. We do not track or store when you receive them.

## Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect device information and an advertising identifier (Advertising ID) to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

For more information about how Google uses your data, please visit:  
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in future app updates.
